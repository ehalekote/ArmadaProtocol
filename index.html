Built For Bundling

Armada is a permissionless protocol for the creation and management of bundles of ERC20 and ERC721 tokens through a higher order primitive. The Armada protocol consists of two layers: a base bundling layer for Armada bundle primitives, and a developer ecosystem driven module layer. Armada can simplify and abstract away ownership and management of multiple assets for all contexts, use cases, and asset types.
