Built For Sharing

Armada is a permissionless protocol for easily sharing ownership of tokenized assets, including video game items, real estate, and everything in between.

We envision a world where the barriers preventing people from owning or investing in typically inaccessible and illiquid assets no longer exist. Armada seeks to foster global financial inclusion by empowering any person, regardless of wealth, to easily buy into a fraction of a tokenized asset.
